Introduction

Ladder game is a lottery game based on the popular Korean game – Sadaritagi. The game is played with a starting point, an ending point or goal, and a ladder to climb on. The game randomly chooses left or right to be the start and the length of the ladder – 3 or 4 legs.

How to Play

At the start of a draw, the ladder is covered to hide the number of legs.
The member can bet on any or all of the different bet types before the draw starts – starting point, number of legs, goal, and combination of goal and number of legs.


Once the draw starts, the ladder is revealed. The game will then pick a starting point then climb down the ladder to reach the Odd or Even goal at the end. When climbing the ladder, when the game encounters a horizontal line, the game must go through it to get to another vertical line and continue downwards until it reaches the goal.


Wager can be placed on the explained bet types below.
The official draw result timing strictly corresponds with the respective region where the game extracted from. The management reserved the rights to cancel or void draw in situation as follow:

  • The official draw result is unobtainable for an extended period of time
  • The official draw result is published before the system is closed for betting Bet Types

Choose a starting point where the ladder will crawl.
Left : The ladder starts on the left.
Right : The ladder starts on the right.

Choose an ending point where the ladder will end.
