Slots Tournament

  1. Slots Tournament is available for all registered members only.
  2. Invitation to participate in exclusive Slots Tournament is rewarded to registered members who are chosen to compete. Members who are not invited to participate are not eligible to compete for leaderboard points.
  3. Tournament invitation cannot be transferred to other accounts or other members.
  4. If member fails to participate during the tournament period, tournament invitation is forfeited.
  5. Tournament invitation cannot be brought forward nor can be exchanged for cash or credit.
  6. Any member that is invited to the tournament will automatically be qualified to earn points for the leaderboard ranking and will be awarded points once member placed bets that meet the required bets and minimum hand.
  7. Tournament is only available on specific game or selection of games. All bets on other non-qualified games cannot be transferred.
  8. Only bets placed during the tournament period qualify for tournament points. Bets before or after the tournament period cannot be transferred.
  9. If two or more players collect the same number of points, then the player with the higher total bet amount will rank higher. If the total bet amount among these players is the same, the rankings are determined in a random order.
  10. The prizes which can be won are non-transferable.
