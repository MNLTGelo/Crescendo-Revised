P2P VIP Level

VIP Level

There is 6 level of VIP level in P2P games. It start from the lowest Earth Dragon to highest Gold Dragon.
The game will start from the lowest level ( Earth Dragon)
To be upgraded to a higher level or to retain the level, player must have and/or maintain pre-requisite VPP points.

Icon Level Name Monthly VPP Required
Earth Dragon -
Fire Dragon To be upgraded to Fire Dragon, "750 amount of VPP points within 60 days "
To be retained in Fire Dragon, "750 amount of VPP points within 60 days "
Water Dragon To be upgraded to Water Dragon, "3,000 amount of VPP points within 60 days "
To be retained in Water Dragon, "2,000 amount of VPP points within 60 days "
Wind Dragon To be upgraded to Wind Dragon, "7,500 amount of VPP points within 60 days "
To be retained in Wind Dragon, "5,000 amount of VPP points within 60 days "
Light Dragon To be upgraded to Light Dragon, " 100,000 amount of VPP points within 365 days "
To be retained in Light Dragon, "70,000 amount of VPP points within 365 days "
To be upgraded to Gold Dragon, “By Invitation Only”
Gold Dragon Not required retention point. Level will not be downgraded and fixed on Dragon level.
- Any cancelation of Sit & Go and Schedule Tournament will be not be calculated for rake and VPP points.
- Player able to upgrade multiple VIP levels in 60 days.

Earning VPP Points
Player can start earn VPP points by playing in cash table and cash tournament (Sit & Go and Scheduled) based on the amount of bet that player contribute on the winning pot. VPPs will be reflected upon completion of the game round.
For every RMB 1,- on rake contribution, 5.5 VPPs are rewarded to player.

RMB 1,- 5.5 VPPs
On cash table , for every RMB 1,- on rake contribution, 5.5 VPPs are rewarded to player.

Rake contribution = Total bet x % Rake
* rake percentage varied based on game

Example:

Player Rake Contribute 3% VPP
Player A , total bet RMB 20,- win 0.60 (20 RMB x 3%) 3.3 (0.60 RMB x 5.5 VPP)
Player B , total bet RMB 20,- lose 0.6 3.3
Player C , total bet RMB 10,- Fold Lose 0.3 1.65
On tournament, for every RMB1,- on tournament entry fee, 5.5 VPPs are rewarded to player.

Example:

Player Buy-In Fee VPP
Player A , Join Tournament 15 3 3 x 5.5 VPP= 16.5 VPPs
Player B , Join Tournament Ticket 0 0 x 5.5 VPP= 0 VPPs
At the end of game round each player will receive respective VPP

Spin The Wheel
On P2P games, the VIP player has an advantage on the spin-the-wheel feature.
Spin the wheel feature enable players to have chances to win cash prize. Number of chance on daily depends on member VIP level.
Spin the wheel has Daily Prize Pool for players to participate and it will be reset daily.

VIP Level Max. Spin
Earth Dragon -
Fire Dragon 3 Times
Water Dragon 4 Times
Wind Dragon 5 Times
Light Dragon 6 Times
Gold Dragon Unlimited

Texas Mahjong has additional previlege from VIP level in which earn Points With Points (PWP).
In addition to earning VPP points, players earn FPP (Frequent Player Points) Points as well.
FPP points can be used to complete the mission to win prizes. Terms and conditions applied
