Player-to-Player (P2P) Terms and Conditions

Buy-in and Auto Buy-in
When entering the cash table a player must have at least minimum buy-in. Players are permitted to acquire additional chips only between hands. If players run out of chips during a hand, it is considered “all-in”.

Auto Buy-in is a feature on the game where the player's start chip is auto top-up within the range of the min and the max buy-in amount by deducting the amount from the player's main balance. The feature must be activated/deactivated by the player manually on the game interface. By activating the feature, players are aware of the risk exposure during the gameplay.
Players are not allowed to move funds from the table during a playing session.

Cash Table Posting Fee
A player joining a P2P games cash table is usually required to pay an initial fee, equal to the table’s lower limit stake or small blind. This initial fee follows the same rules as the blind bets (initial mandatory bets, typically placed in the pot by the first two players to the left of the dealer in games and allows the new player to sit-in at the table and join the game.

Missed Blind Bets
Cash table game, a player will be sit-out automatically from the game when no action was taken by the player when the game starts upon receiving the initial hand cards/tiles. The respective player then will be automatically exited from the table game once the game round ends.

Tie Hands
If two or more active players reach a showdown at the table, the pot (the accumulated amount of chips in the center of the table, being contested by the active players) will be split evenly between those players.

Play Time Limit
To ensure a fair and enjoyable playing experience, players must act within a reasonable time limit that varies per table, per game variant, and/or per game stage (i.e. the length of time that has elapsed in a given tournament, etc..).

The time limits are enforced to maintain a reasonable speed of gameplay. Players who fail to act within the set time limit will have their hand automatically folded and will forfeit any amounts that he/she has previously wagered during that hand.

Leave Table
To ensure the best possible gaming experience for all, players are not allowed to leave the table once the game round started on the cash game table. Players however allow to leave the cash table after one game round (one hand). The policy applies to all cash game variants and tables.

Rake and Fees
Cash table applies a commission charge (often known as “rake”) to each pot at cash game tables. To find out more details on the rake collected at the respective game rules.

In tournaments, the cash table does not levy a rake but typically collects a tournament fee instead. The amount is often a set percentage of the tournament buy-in that show on the tournament lobby for each event.

Table Etiquette
The normal courtesies are expected at any P2P table game. Intimidation or harassment of other players at the table will not be tolerated; Do not spam emojis or engage in “shouting matches” with other players at the table; Lagging is a natural occurrence in the internet age. As it is unavoidable, please be polite when notifying a player who is lagging.
