Player-to-Player (P2P) Tournament

Tournament Participation

  1. Player to player (P2P) Tournament is available for all registered players only.
  2. Players can participate in a tournament by.
    3. - Invitation through ticket reward by the tournament organizer
    - Purchase tournaments buy-in
  3. Tournament by Invitation:
    4. It is an exclusive tournament that rewarded for registered players that are chosen by the tournament organizer to compete. Players who are not invited to participate are not eligible to compete for the leaderboard. Tournament invitations cannot be transferred to other accounts or other players.
    If a player fails to participate during the tournament period, the tournament invitation is forfeited. Tournament invitations cannot be brought forward nor can be exchanged for cash or credit.
  4. Tournament by Buy-In Purchase
    5. It is an open tournament for any registered players able to participate simply by purchasing the buy-in that indicates on the lobby of the tournament event. Depending on the type of tournament, buy-in generally can be part of the prize pool.
    **Buy-in includes tournament fees.
  5. Tournament is available on the game specified by the tournament organizer.
  6. Gameplay Interactive tournaments allow players to un-register before the event begins. The exact time at which un-registration closes may vary from event to event. Registration and un-registration times may vary between tournaments of different types. Not all tournaments allow un-registration, and some tournaments do not allow players to un-register via giveaway tournaments. Gameplay Interactive reserves the right to alter registration and un-registration times without prior notice.

Tournament Start Time
All tournaments will begin promptly at the scheduled time stated in the tournament lobby. reserves the right to delay or cancel any tournament without prior notice. Seats are assigned to players at random.

Tournament Elimination
The tournament will end when a single player accumulates all the winning chips in the play. The eliminations depend on the number of registered players to participate in the tournament.

The Gameplay Interactive system will break the table based on the number of registered participants and randomly assign the player’s seat.

The winner on the table will be reassigned randomly to a new table and the process will continue until the last table. In the last tournament table, the player that accumulates the winning chips from other tournament participants is the winner.

Tournament Ranking or Leaderboard
Tournament participants that win the tournament will be displayed on the tournament ranking or leaderboard with the prize won.

Tournament Disconnects and Sitting Out Policy
By participating in a tournament, players accept the risk of internet disconnection, due to problems with the connection between their device(s) and the servers, lag or freeze, or some other problem in the player’s device(s) or how that device accesses the internet.

Gameplay Interactive does not accept any responsibility for a player disconnect except in the case of a server crash. While each user is responsible for his or her own internet connection, Gameplay Interactive tries to protect players who are disconnected during the final stages of a real-money tournament, by allowing extra time to reconnect.

If a player times out during a hand, whether connected or disconnected, his or her hand will be folded if facing action or may be checked if facing no action.

If a player is not connected before a hand starts, he or she will be dealt cards, and a blind and/or ante will be posted. There is no rule against a single player choosing to sit out; the player doing so will continue to have blinds and antes posted and cards dealt.

Two or more players may not make any agreement to sit out simultaneously, whether at the same table or different tables; such actions may fall under the gameplay collusion policy.

Hands-in-play
Intentionally or inadvertently influencing the progression of a hand that is in play or the action of its participants by way of discussion, use of emoji, or utilization of video cam or any similar app, is strictly prohibited and may result in temporary or permanent account suspension.

Tournament Prize Award
Prizes are awarded as stated in the tournament lobby, except when a deal is made, or the tournament is canceled. The prize structure used in any given tournament depends on several factors including the number of entrants and the number of players per table. The prizes awarded in any given tournament are not finalized until registration has closed and the rebuy and/or add-on period is over.

Gameplay Interactive reserves the right (at any time) to modify the prizes awarded in any given tournament. Further, Gameplay Interactive reserves the right to award a monetary prize of equal or greater value as the main prize in place of the main tournament prize, at its sole discretion.

If two or more players win the tournament the prize pool will be divided equally among the winning players. The prizes which can be won are non-transferable. ** In all instances where a player’s winnings include a fraction of a cent, Gameplay Interactive will round down such player’s winnings to the nearest cent.

Refund
In the event of Gameplay Interactive servers crashing or a Gameplay Interactive unscheduled downtime happening, Gameplay Interactive shall refund players as described below. Gameplay Interactive, in its sole discretion, shall decide otherwise for any other events disrupting play caused by the Licensee or the Licensee’s players.

For Cash Games, all hands in progress will be refunded back. Each player’s chip count will be reset to the amount at the beginning of the hand.

For tournaments, there are possibilities depending on the status of the Tournament:
  1. If the tournament has not started, Gameplay Interactive shall refund all buy-ins and fees to the registered players.
  2. If the tournament did not meet the requirement in terms of the minimum number of players and/or the registration time is over all the buy-ins and feed are to be refunded.
  3. If the ongoing tournament is disrupted, Gameplay Interactive reserves the right to have a full or partial refund of the affected tournament. Please note that when calculating tournament refunds, Gameplay Interactive will determine the moment the disruption began and use the state of play in any given tournament immediately prior to this disruption as a basis for calculating the refunds. Not all players may experience disruption at the same time.
