Introduction
Andar Bahar is table game that getting popular around the world. The game origin from south east asia with simple game play of predicting the middle card will appear on Andar or Bahar side.

On every new round, a standard 52-card deck is used.

How to Play
1. At the start of the draw, place your bet on your selected bet types.
2. If you placing bet on Middle card - bet types , bet will be closed before the middle card is drawn.
3. You still can place bet on the main game bet types until the timer runs out.
4. when the timer runs out, cards will be drawn randomly to Andar and Bahar side until matching card with middle card appear on either side.
5. If result match, you win else lose.

In Andar Bahar game , the main goal is for player to predict the middle card will appear on Andar side or Bahar side.

The game comprise of 2 sessions, the pre-game session and the main game session.

The Pre-Game Session :
Player to predict Middle Card result.
The session start when draw is open before the first card (middle card) drawn by dealer.
Once Dealer drawn the middle card , its end of the pre-game session.

Main Game Session:
Player to predict which side the matching middle card will appear. Will it be on Andar or Bahar?When the timer runs out, dealer will begin dealing single card face up on each side.

IF middle card is Black color (Spade or Club suit), the first card drawn to Andar
IF middle card is Red color (Heart or Diamond suit), the first card drawn to Bahar

This will continue until the same value card as middle card appear. Once the matching card appear on either side, the game ends.

Bet Types

Pre- Game : Middle Card
Color(Black | Red)
Bet on middle card result in black / red color cards, if match player win else lose.
Example : player bet on Red . Middle card result is Player Wins.

Suit (Spade| Heart | Club | Diamond)
Bet on middle card result in Spade/Heart/ Club or Diamond suit, if match player win else lose. Example : player bet on Heart . Middle card result is Player Wins.

Card Value (Above | Exact | Below)
Bet on middle card result in above / Exact / Below 8, if match player win else lose.
Example : player bet on card value above 8 . Middle card result is Player Wins.

** note: Ace card rank and counts as 13th card.

Main Game

Number of cards Drawn
Bet on total cards drawn until matching card with middle card appear.
Example :
Middle card Result Player bet on 6 -10 range.
Andar Result : A-heart, 10 Spade, 3 Heart, 2 club,-
Bahar Result : 6 Club , 4 Heart, 3 Club, 7 Heart
Total card drawn 8. Player wins.
** note : card counted including the matching card with middle card.
