Blossom Garden is a 3x5 collapsing reels video slot game with 30 fixed paylines. Three to five matching symbols along any payline from leftmost reel to right has a corresponding win amount. The game has two features: Instant Win and Mystical Free Spins.

The entire reel where there is a winning symbol would be removed and new symbols will fill the gaps on those reels. The reels are reassessed for new winning combinations and are paid accordingly. This process continues until there are no more winning combinations after the replacement of all the symbols on the reel where there was a winning combination.





Game Symbols

Symbol combination payouts are listed on the paytable. Win amount is computed by multiplying the payout value by the line bet – except for the scatter payout which is multiplied by total bet.

High-paying Symbols

Low-paying Symbols

Wild Symbol

Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols on a possible win line EXCEPT for the Scatter symbol. The Wild symbol would be available on ALL reels. It is available during normal game and during free spins.


Only the highest payout would be paid.Payouts with the wild symbol will be evaluated to see which combination would award the highest pay – either the Wild symbol payout or the combination payout wherein the wild would replace the other symbols.



Example:
Symbols on the reel (MAIN GAME): Wild – Wild – Wild – Ten – Ten
Line bet: USD 0.04 , Total bet: USD 1.20
Line payout:
5-of-a-kind payout for Ten symbol - 50 x USD 0.04 (line bet) = USD 2.00
3-of-a-kind payout for Wild symbol - 150 x USD 0.04 (line bet) = USD 1.20



Player is paid with the highest pay – which is the Ten Symbol payout USD 2.00

Scatter Symbol

The Scatter symbol triggers the mystical free spins feature. Scatter symbols appear on all reels during normal game.

Getting 3 Scatter symbols anywhere on reels 2, 3, and 4 during a normal game would award the player with the default 14 free spins. Three scatter symbols also pay 1x the total bet.

Instant Wins

Instant win triggers randomly on the game after either five or three reels explode from getting 3-of-a-kind or 5-of-a-kind winning combinations (except scatter wins). At the end of the instant win, a new set of symbols will fill in the reels and will be reevaluated for any winning combination.

It can be awarded with range of x5, x8, x10, x20, x30, x40, x50, x60, x100, x200, or x500. Instant win can be triggered both on main game and during free spins.

Mystical Free Spins

Mystical free spins are triggered from the main game when 3 or more Scatter symbols appear on Reels 2, 3, and 4. The player will be awarded the default 14 mystical free spins.

Free spins would use the same lines bet that triggered the feature. Free spins cannot be retriggered. During free spins, Instant Win could be triggered.

How To Play
  1. Choose the Total Bet per round by selecting a Line Bet value to be applied on each paylines.
  2. To start the round, click the SPIN button.
  3. Once the reels stop, the combination of symbols displayed determine the payout as could be seen on the paytable. Check the total win on the WIN field.

BET
The total bet is shown in TOTAL BET display. Player could change the bet by changing the bet value (increase or decrease) which would be applied on all the number of paylines.

PAYLINES OR PAY WAYS & PAYTABLE
The number of game paylines or pay ways is fixed. The game payline or pay ways patterns, and the paytable that show the winning symbol combination payout are displayed by clicking on the “i” button.

WIN
Any total win in the game round will be displayed on the WIN field. The win amount for each combination could be seen on the info table where the paytable, paylines, and feature information are. Only the highest payline win will be paid.

If the game has a feature, all winnings from features triggered are added to the payline or pay way total win. If the game has a feature, all winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round/spin and NOT at the end of the feature triggered.

For games with Free Spin feature, each free spin is played with the same line bet chosen during the game round that triggered it. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round or feature.

Reconnecting Games
If in the middle of the main game the player disconnected from the Internet, the spin will be automatically completed. Once the player re-opens the game, it will start from the very beginning. Players previous wins will be paid. To check the previous outcome, click on History button after login.

If the main game is disconnected from the Internet during a spin using the Auto Spin function, the spin will be automatically completed, but further spins will not automatically start. The game’s previous wins will be paid. To check the previous outcome, click on History button after login.

If the game is disconnected from the Internet during the Free Spins, Bonus Game and/or other feature, the player will be automatically directed back to the game after login to continue the game. Players previous wins will be paid. To check the previous outcome, click on History button after login.

Do note that a malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Frost Dragon is a 3x5 collapsing reels video slot game with 20 fixed paylines. Three to five matching symbols along any payline from leftmost reel to right has a corresponding win amount. The game has two features: Downrush Reel Mode and Free Spins.

During Downrush Reel Mode, winning symbols are removed from the reels. Existing and new symbols from the top fall down in sequence to fill in the gaps. The reels are reassessed for new winning combinations and are paid accordingly. This process continues until there are no more winning combinations after replacement of symbols. Every collapse during Downrush Reel Mode has a corresponding multiplier which increases for every consecutive collapse.



Game Symbol

Symbol combination payouts are listed on the paytable. Win amount is computed by multiplying the payout value by the line bet – except for the scatter payout which is multiplied by total bet.

High-paying Symbols

Wild Symbol
Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols on a possible win line EXCEPT for the Scatter symbol.

The Wild symbol would be available on Reels 2, 3, and 4. Stacked Wilds appear on Reel 3 for more chances to form winning combinations.

Scatter Symbol
Three scatter symbols from leftmost to right trigger the Free Spins Feature.

Scatter symbols would be available on Reels 1, 2, and 3.

Free Spins

The Free Spins feature could be triggered by getting 3 scatter symbols from left to right which would reward.Free spins would use the same lines bet that triggered the feature. Free spin feature can be re-triggered. During free spins, Downrush Reel Mode is enabled and has higher multipliers than the main game.

Downrush Reel is enabled during main game and free spins and can award up to x1, x2, x3, and x5 multiplier for main game, and x3, x6, x9, and x15 multiplier for free spins. Each succeeding collapse will trigger an increase on the multiplier and will max out at x5 for main game and x15 for free spins. Below are the rules for the Downrush Reel:

Downrush Reel Mode

The starting multiplier is x1 for main game and x3 for free spins. Once the player wins a game, the winning symbols would be paid and this is considered the first collapse. The multiplier is set from x1 to x2 for main game, and x3 to x6 for free spins.

  1. If the new symbols form a new winning combination, this is considered the second collapse and the multiplier will increase. For every consecutive collapse, the multiplier would increase by a step (i.e. from x2 to x3 for main game and x6 to x9 for free spins).
  2. Once the player gets the 3rd collapse, the multiplier is set to the maximum x5 for main game, and x15 for free spins. Any collapse after that would get the same multiplier.
  3. If there are no winning combinations left, the game proceeds to the next spin and the multiplier will reset back to x1 for main game, and x3 for free spins.
  4. If the player gets another winning combination on the next spin, the multiplier will move up by a step, and the same rules as written above would be followed.

Example:
Main Game
Line bet: USD 1.00
Total bet: USD 20.00
SPIN : 3-of-a-kind symbol

10 x USD 1.00 (line bet) x 1 (multiplier) = USD 10.00 COLLAPSE 1: 3-of-a-kind symbol

3 x USD 1.00 (line bet) x 2 (multiplier) = USD 6.00COLLAPSE 2: 3-of-a-kind symbol

50 x USD 1.00 (line bet) x 3 (multiplier) = USD 50.00COLLAPSE 3: 3-of-a-kind symbol>

3 x USD 1.00 (line bet) x 3 (multiplier) = USD 9.00COLLAPSE 4: No new winning combinations are evaluated. Multiplier reset to x1 Multiplier.

Total win for the Spin is: USD 66.00

If the main game is disconnected from the Internet during a spin using the Auto Spin function, the spin will be automatically completed, but further spins will not automatically start. The game’s previous wins will be paid. To check the previous outcome, click on History button after login.

If the game is disconnected from the Internet during the Free Spins, Bonus Game and/or other feature, the player will be automatically directed back to the game after login to continue the game. Players previous wins will be paid. To check the previous outcome, click on History button after login.

Do note that a malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Genie Luck is a 40 bet-line 3x4x4x4x3 video slot game. Three to five matching symbols along any payways from leftmost reel to right has a corresponding win amount during main game. During free spins, winning combinations are evaluated both ways – from leftmost reel reel to right and rightmost reel reel to left. The game has two features: Genie Wish Respin and Free Spins.


Game Symbols

Symbol combination payouts are listed on the paytable. Win amount is computed by multiplying the payout value by the line bet – except for the scatter payout which is multiplied by total bet.

High-paying Symbols
Low-paying Symbols

Wild Symbol
The Wild symbol awards combination payouts and also substitutes for all symbols on a possible win line EXCEPT for the Scatter symbol The Wild symbol appears on ALL reels and may appear as either single wild or stacked wilds.

Wild payouts would be awarded when Wilds are on a winning payline. During free spins, all wild payouts would be done both ways (from leftmost reel to right and rightmost reel to left).

Example:
Symbols on the reel (FREE SPINS): Wild – Wild – Wild – Wild – Wild
Line bet: USD 0.04
Total bet: USD 0.16
Wild payout (Leftmost reel to right): 400 (5-of-a-kind) x USD 0.04 (line bet) = USD 16.00
Wild payout (Rightmost reel to left): 400 (5-of-a-kind) x USD 0.04 (line bet) = USD 16.00
Total payout: USD 32.00
Since free spins pay both ways, the player is awarded with two wild symbol payouts – one from leftmost reel to right, and one from rightmost reel to left.

Example:
Symbols on the reel (FREE SPINS): Wild – Wild – Wild – Jack – Jack
Line bet: USD 0.04
Total bet: USD 0.16
Wild payout (Leftmost reel to right): 40 (3-of-a-kind) x USD 0.04 (line bet) = USD 1.60
Line payout (Rightmost reel to left): 12 (5-of-a-kind) x USD 0.04 (line bet) = USD 0.48
Total payout: USD 2.08
The player is only awarded with one wild symbol payout because the pay is from rightmost reel to left and the wild symbol is not on the rightmost reel. The pay from rightmost reel to left is the 5-of-a-kind symbol payout for the Jack symbol instead.

Only the highest payout would be paid..Payouts with the wild symbol will be evaluated to see which combination would award the highest pay – either the Wild symbol payout or the combination payout wherein the wild would replace the other symbols.

Example:
Symbols on the reel (MAIN GAME): Wild – Wild – Genie – Genie – Genie
Line bet: USD 0.04
Total bet: USD 0.16
Line payout: 120 (5-of-a-kind payout for Genie symbol) x USD 0.04 (line bet) = USD 4.80
The Wild symbol payout would be 6 x line bet and the payout if the Wild replaces the Genie symbol would be 120 x line bet. So the player is paid with the highest pay for Genie symbol instead of the Wild payout.

Example:
Symbols on the reel (FREE SPINS): Wild – Wild – Genie – Genie – Genie
Line bet: USD 0.04
Total bet: USD 0.16
Line payout (Leftmost reel to right): 120 (5-of-a-kind payout for Genie symbol) x USD 0.04 (line bet) = USD 4.80
Line payout (Rightmost reel to left): 120 (5-of-a-kind payout for Genie symbol) x USD 0.04 (line bet) = USD 4.80

The Wild symbol payout would be 6 x line bet and the payout if the Wild replaces the Genie symbol would be 120 x line bet. So the player is paid with the highest pay for Genie symbol instead of the Wild payout. Since this is won during free spins, the combination is paid twice: one for leftmost reel to right and one for rightmost reel to left.

Example:

Symbols on the reel (MAIN GAME): Wild – Wild – Wild – Turban – Turban

Line bet: USD 0.04

Total bet: USD 0.16

Line payout: 40 (3-of-a-kind payout for Wild symbol) x USD 0.04 (line bet) = USD 1.60

The Wild symbol payout is 40 x line bet for three of a kind. If the Wild replaces the Turban symbol, the payout would be 30 x line bet for 5-of-a-kind payout. So the player is paid with the Wild payout instead of the Turban symbol payout.

Scatter Symbol

Scatter symbols appear on reels 2, 3, and 4 only, and are available during main game and free spins. Three (3) Scatter symbols awards the default
10 free spins.

Genie symbol

The Genie symbol awards combination payouts.


During main game, a full stack of genie symbols on Reel 1 will trigger respins in main game. Stacked Genie symbols on Reel 5 will NOT trigger the Genie Wish Respin mode.


During free spins, stacked genies on either Reel 1 or Reel 5 will trigger the Genie Wish Respin mode. Details on this are on Genie Wish Respin During Free Spins section.

Genie Wish Respin


On Main game, a full stack of Genie symbols appearing on Reel 1 awards three (3) respins.

During Genie Wish Respin, all Genie and Wildsymbols are locked on the reels. The first and second respin will only be for
collecting Genie and Wild symbols and no payout computation would be done yet. On the third respin, the reel would be evaluated for all wins
on all symbol combinations and would be paid out accordingly.


Respin payways during the main game is from leftmost reel to right.

Free Spins

Three Scattersymbols on reels 2, 3 and 4 trigger the Free Game Feature and awards 10 free spins. Free spins would use the same lines
bet that triggered the feature. Free spin feature can be re-triggered by getting additional 3 scatter symbols during free spins. Symbol payouts
are computed both ways (from leftmost reel reel to right and rightmost reel reel to left).

Genie Wish Respin During Free Spins
During Free Spins, a full stack of Genie symbols on either Reel 1 or Reel 5 awards 3 respins. The Genie symbol will mirror to the opposite reel direction
from either Reel 1 to Reel 5, or from Reel 5 to Reel 1.



Genie and Wild symbols would be locked on the reels and all 3 respins would be played before continuing to the remaining free spins. Genie Wish Respins triggered during Free Spins would also have payouts computed both ways (from leftmost reel reel to right and rightmost reel reel to left).

How To Play
  1. Choose the Total Bet per round by selecting a Line Bet value to be applied on each paylines.
  2. To start the round, click the SPIN button.
  3. Once the reels stop, the combination of symbols displayed determine the payout as could be seen on the paytable. Check the total win on the WIN field.

BET
The total bet is shown in TOTAL BET display. Player could change the bet by changing the bet value (increase or decrease) which would be applied on all the number of paylines.

PAYLINES OR PAY WAYS & PAYTABLE
The number of game paylines or pay ways is fixed. The game payline or pay ways patterns, and the paytable that show the winning symbol combination payout are displayed by clicking on the “i” button.

WIN
Any total win in the game round will be displayed on the WIN field. The win amount for each combination could be seen on the info table where the paytable, paylines, and feature information are. Only the highest payline win will be paid.

If the game has a feature, all winnings from features triggered are added to the payline or pay way total win. If the game has a feature, all winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round/spin and NOT at the end of the feature triggered.

For games with Free Spin feature, each free spin is played with the same line bet chosen during the game round that triggered it. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round or feature.
