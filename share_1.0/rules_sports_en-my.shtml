American Football

American Football

All games must start on the scheduled date (local time) for bets to have action. Abandoned or postponed matches are void unless rearranged and played on the same date (local time) or stated otherwise in the rules. An exception is made if incorrect kick off time is announced on our website. All bets on the game include overtime scoring unless stated otherwise. Games must go at least 55 minutes of play for bets to have action. If a game is suspended before 55 complete minutes are played, all bets on the outcome of the game are refunded unless otherwise stated, except for bets on any markets that have been unconditionally determined. In 2-way markets push rules apply unless otherwise stated below. Stakes on single bets are returned and in multiples/parlays the selection is treated as a non-runner. If a match venue is changed, bets already placed will stand providing the home team is still designated as such. If the home and away team for a listed match are reversed, then bets based on the original listing will be void.

1.3 Buying points

Buying points allows you to change the point-spread or the game total of an American Football game. You can move the point-spread in order to get more points when betting on the underdog, and fewer points when betting on the favorite. You can move the total in order to get a higher total when betting on under or a lower total if betting on over.

1.4 Spread

Predict the winner of the game, applying the given spread (handicap).

1.5 Winner

Predict the winner of the game. Bets include overtime if played. If the game ends in a draw, 2Way bets on Winner will be void.

1.6 Over/Under / Total Points

Predict if the points scored by both teams will be over or under a given number.

1.7 1st Half

Bets settled on the first half result only. Bets will be void if the match is abandoned before half time. If a match is abandoned during the second half then all first half bets are still valid.

1.8 2nd Half

Bets settled on the second half result only include overtime.

1.9 1st/2nd/3rd/ Quarter

Bets settled on the quarters result only. If the entire game is not completed, wagers on quarters will have action in case the relevant period was completed.

1.10 4th Quarter

Bets settled on the 4th quarter result only include overtime (unless otherwise stated).

1.11 Halftime/Fulltime

Predict the result of a match at halftime and fulltime. Example: If you chose 1/2, you bet on the home team to lead in the first half and the away team to win the game. Bets on Halftime/Fulltime exclude overtime.

1.12 Odd/Even

A prediction of whether the total number of accumulated points in a relevant period - quarter/half/game, will add up to an odd or even number.

1.13 First to score/Last to score

First/Last team to score means betting on which team will score the first or last point in a match. If an event is abandoned after a point has been scored, then all bets on First team to score will stand, while bets on Last team to score will be void. If an event is abandoned without any points being scored, then all wagers on First/Last team to score will be void.

1.14 Total team points

"Total team points" is similar to Over/Under Wager. Win/loss is determined by the points accumulated by a named team.

1.15 To Qualify

Should a team be disqualified from the tournament prior to the match, and a bye is awarded, then all qualification bets will be void.

1.16 Over Time

Predict whether the game will go to overtime or not.

1.17 Total Team Odd/Even

A prediction of whether the points scored by a named team will add up to an odd or even number.

1.18 Highest Scoring Half/Quarter

Highest Half/Quarter bets include overtime. Push rules apply.

1.19 Touchdowns

"Touchdowns" is similar to Over/Under Wager. Win/loss is determined by the number of Touchdowns accumulated by both teams.

1.20 Touchdown Scorers

Bets on players not taking part in the match will be void. If a player doesn't play until First Touchdown is scored, then bets on him regarding First Touchdown will be void. If a match is abandoned, all bets will be void, unless a Touchdown is already scored. Then all bets for First Touchdown scorer will stand, Last and Anytime Touchdown scorers will be void.

1.21 Winning Margin

Predict the team to win the match and the margin of victory. Overtime is included (if played).

1.22 Race to 10

Predict which team will be the first to score 10 points in the game. If the game is abandoned or finishes before 10 points are scored by any team, bets will be void.
