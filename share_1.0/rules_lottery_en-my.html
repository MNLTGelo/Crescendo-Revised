Fast 3

Introduction
Fast 3 is a lottery game played using 3 dice. Each draw has 3 winning numbers - from 1 to 6. Members place bet on the following bet types explained below.

The management reserves the rights to cancel or void draws in situation as follows:

  • The official draw is unobtainable for an extended period of time
  • The official draw result is published before the system is closed for betting

How to Play
At the start of the draw, member places bet on one or more of the following bet types. At the start of the draw, three dice will roll & reveal the results. Member wins if his bet matches the results.

Bet Types
Every draw result contains 3 dice and their significant position in the order. There are different bet types a member can bet on for all and each of the result. The details of all the bet types are as discussed here:

Three Identical

Three of a Kind
How to Play - Select a group of triple numbers from 111 - 666 to make 1 bet.
How to Win - Result matches the selected triple numbers.

Bet: [111]
Result: [111] is a win

How to Win - Result is a triple number.

GPI Lottery

Introduction
GPI Lottery is lottery game played by matching numbers based on 4 sets of number results. Wager can be placed on the explained bet types below.

The official draw result time strictly corresponds with the respective region where the results are obtained.

The management reserves the rights to cancel or void draws in situation as follows:
The official draw is unobtainable for an extended period of time
The official draw result is published before the system is closed for betting

How to Play
The game is played by selecting bet depending on the 4 sets of results, grouped as:

  • Category 1 – a 6-digit number, called the First Prize
  • Category 2 – two 3-digit numbers, called the First 3 Digits
  • Category 3 – two 3-digit numbers, called the Last 3 Digits
  • Category 4 – a 2-digit number, called the 2 Digits

For each category, a set of digits from 0 to 9 will be drawn in order. Members then have the option to wager on the explained bet types below for one or more of the categories.

 

Categories & Bet Types per Category
There are four categories for the draw results of each draw.

A 6-digit number is drawn for this category. Members can bet on the last three digits of this result.

The official draw result timing strictly corresponds with the respective region where the game extracted from. The management reserved the rights to cancel or void draw in situation as follow:

The official draw result is unobtainable for an extended period of time

Thai Lottery

Introduction
Thai Lottery is based on the lottery game issued by the Thai Government Lottery Office. The draw is conducted on the 1st and 16th of each month. Thai Lottery game opens 6 days before the draw date.

The official draw result time strictly corresponds with the Thai Government Lottery Office draw time.

The management reserves the rights to cancel or void draws in situation as follows:
The official draw is unobtainable for an extended period of time
The official draw result is published before the system is closed for betting

How to Play
The game is played by selecting bet depending on the 4 sets of results, grouped as:

  • Category 1 – a 6-digit number, called the First Prize
  • Category 2 – two 3-digit numbers, called the First 3 Digits
  • Category 3 – two 3-digit numbers, called the Last 3 Digits
  • Category 4 – a 2-digit number, called the 2 Digits

For each category, a set of digits from 0 to 9 will be drawn in order. Members then have the option to wager on the explained bet types below for one or more of the categories.

 

Categories & Bet Types per Category
There are four categories for the draw results of each draw.


Category 1 - “First Prize”
A 6-digit number is drawn for this category. Members can bet on the last three digits of this result.


Bet Types:
Exact 3
Member selects 3-digit number between 000-999. The 3 numbers must match the last three digits of the result in exact order.

Example:
Member wins when member bets on [224]

Example:
Member wins when member bets on any: [224], [242], [422]

Member selects 2-digit number between 00-99. The 2 numbers must match the last two digits of the result in exact order.

Example:
Member wins when member bets on [24].

Any 1
Member selects 1-digit number between 0-9. The number must appear in any of the last three digits of the result.

Example:
Member wins when member bets on any: [2], [4]

BSOE (Big Small Odd Even)
Member bets on the sum of the last three digits of the result.
Big: Sum is > 13
Small: Sum is <= 13
Odd: Sum is Odd number
Even: Sum is Even number
